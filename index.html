Our Story

Exploding onto the scene in late 2013 at the annual SuperKarts! USA (SKUSA) SuperNationals, BENIK Kart has quickly made a name in the karting world. Starting off small and focusing on image and results, BENIK Kart has grown to compete along side other manufactures. From an early stage having been seen on the top step of the podium at multiple high profile events throughout North America BENIK Kart has gained momentum and gone on to dominate the pinnacle of all karting events in North America.
